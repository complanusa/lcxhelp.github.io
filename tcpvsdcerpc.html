Differences between the "TCP" and "DCERPC" WinPCS versions.

The original client-server "32-bit" WinPCS system uses DCE/RPC over TCP for its communication between the client and server. This works great within a single company network, and it has also been useful for client and server communicating over the Internet at large. It requires a specific TCP port opened for the clients to access the server, and requires an end-to-end connectivity.
